Corporate Events Choreography

We have have rendered our services for some of leading corporate houses in India. The asignments included Staff Incentive Programs, Product Launch, Theme Events, Employee Parties, Award Ceremonies and Annual Day Events. We provide all this for your company. We always present at all events to make sure everything goes well. The client comes first. Choreography is the heart of any production vision and the art of making it come to life, however simple or complex. Our choreographers understand the value of this and are some of the most creative artists of the movement world right now. With a wide range of choreography, we can provide dancers and trainers.Scope of our services includes Choreography (Dance Training) for group of participating staff and support services.

Wedding Choreography

Our dance choreographers, determined to help you in every ‘step’ of your journey. Do you have an engagement party, a wedding sangeet or any other event in which you have to perform a dance routine with a group of people or singularly? If yes, you have come to the right place!. All around the world, weddings are usually one of the most joyous and important events a family experiences. In India, it is no different. Engagement ceremonies, Sangeet Sandhya, Mehendi Ceremonies and Bachelor/Bachelorette/Spinster Parties are characteristically similar in one way. DANCE! No wedding celebration is complete without the element of song and dance. Our team will help you set up performances of a life time that would surely have your relatives, friends and loved ones remembering many years! Not only do we help you in setting up performances for events, but we also create theme based sangeet sandhya events.

  • Engagement ceremonies
  • Sangeet Sandhya
  • Mehendi Ceremonies
  • Bachelor
  • Bachelorette
  • Spinster

Private Events Choreography

If you are looking for professional choreographers for your show/event/functions then please do not hesitate to contact and discuss your requirements. Our dancers are professionally trained and have a wealth of experience. Arpita Step Up Dance Academy will help you execute your vision for this special event with professionalism and grace. Teaching is our passion, and we can work with your dance ability, personality, style and love story to create a first dance, or guide you and your friends and family in an unforgettable performance.
